Welcome to PERM Timeline! We are dedicated to providing the most current processing time information for PERM applications (Form ETA-9089). As immigrants ourselves, we deeply understand the uncertainty and stress associated with this process.
Our platform utilizes the Department of Labor's query system to deliver accurate and timely data on PERM applications submitted after June 2023. This enhancement ensures that you receive the most reliable information to navigate your PERM application process with greater certainty and confidence. For applications submitted prior to June 2023, data was crowd-sourced from shared messages within our Telegram group, reflecting our community's collaborative spirit.

Join our Telegram group (@PermLC) to connect with others, ask your questions, and follow the latest news.

Visit the latest blogs (permtimeline.com/blog) to gain valuable information on the PERM process.

Daily Progress Statistics
  • How to read the chart?
  • It shows the number of cases that have been approved, audited, or denied in the recent days.
  • You can zoom and pan to focus on dates of your interest.
  • You can hide/show bars by clicking on the corresponding legend.
  • Dates in cyan color are weekends or federal holidays.

Number of days for approval in the past 30 days
  • How to read the table?
  • It shows the processing time of the cases that are approved in the past 30 days. For example, if it is 486 days for the 30% percentile, it means that 30% of approved cases in the past 30 days have been pending for 486 days or less.
  • This excludes approval of audited cases.

Priority Date Wise Statistics
  • How to read the chart?
  • It shows the distribution of cases based on their priority date (the date the PERM application was submitted to DOL).
  • You can zoom and pan to focus on dates of your interest.
  • You can hide/show bars by clicking on the corresponding legend.
  • Dates in cyan color are weekends or federal holidays.
